Privacy Policy Last updated: February 2, 2026 Emergency App ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. 1. Information We Collect We collect minimal information necessary to provide emergency services: • User ID: A unique 8-character identifier generated locally on your device • User Name: Optional name you provide for identification in SOS alerts • Push Notification Token: Device token for sending emergency notifications (provided by OneSignal) • Guardian IDs: IDs of trusted contacts you add for emergency alerts • Location Data: GPS coordinates only when you send an SOS alert (optional feature) 2. How We Use Your Information We use the collected information solely for the following purposes: • Sending SOS emergency alerts to your designated guardians • Delivering push notifications • Identifying you in emergency situations • Maintaining the functionality of the app We do NOT: • Sell your data to third parties • Use your data for advertising • Track your location continuously • Share your information except as required for emergency services 3. Data Storage and Security Your data is stored securely: • Local Storage: Guardian information and preferences are stored locally on your device • Cloud Storage: User ID, name, and push tokens are stored on Supabase (encrypted database) • Push Service: Notification tokens are managed by OneSignal for delivering alerts We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. 4. Third-Party Services We use the following third-party services: • Supabase: Database and backend services (Privacy Policy) • OneSignal: Push notification delivery (Privacy Policy) These services may collect technical information necessary for their operation. Please review their privacy policies for details. 5. Location Information Location data is only collected when you: • Explicitly send an SOS alert with location enabled • Grant location permissions in your device settings We do not track your location in the background or when the app is not in use. 6. Children's Privacy Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. 7. Your Rights You have the right to: • Access: Request a copy of your data • Deletion: Request deletion of your data • Correction: Update incorrect information • Opt-out: Disable push notifications in app settings To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email below. 8. Data Retention We retain your data only as long as necessary: • Local data is deleted when you uninstall the app • Server data can be deleted upon request • Inactive accounts may be removed after 12 months of inactivity 9. International Data Transfers Your data may be processed and stored in countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information. 10. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by: • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy • Sending an in-app notification for significant changes Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. 11. Contact Us If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your rights, contact us: Email: emergencyapp.support@proton.me We will respond to your request within 30 days. © 2026 Emergency App. All rights reserved.
